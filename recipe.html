Recipe #2: Gelato

  • Time: 30 Minutes
  • Serves: 1 Person
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 1 cup
sugar 1 cup
vanilla 3 tbs

Step 1: mix milk and sugar

Step 2: slowly add vanilla while you stir together the milk and sugar

Step 3: put the mix in the ice cream maker and add ice cream salt slowly

Step 4: wait 30 minutes and enjoy!

Details

This gelto is vanilla and is very healthy and only 220 calories!
